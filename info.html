Through the centuries, wine has been a social event. From the harvest to the new wine festivals, wine brings people together. It has been our dream to create a facility that brings to reality a wine making experience for all who want to participate. We have learned a lot about what people like in their wine making experience, and that people, like their wine, appreciate a personal and intimate touch. Our focus is on the quality and personal care you and your wine creation deserve. No mass production or factory feels at Harmony Winery, if you’re not happy, we’re not happy. Like people, wine is as individual as its creator. Through your own hands, you will bring to life your own vintage. It starts with a wine tasting session to decide which wine to make. Then we walk you through the process of making your own personalized vintage in our state-of-the-art wine studio. Each vintage will include everything you need: bottles, corks, seals and custom labels. Wine making is an enjoyable experience and custom label wine makes a wonderful gift for many occasions. For the wine lover and wine maker, this is a truly unique experience. Harmony Winery is your one-stop shop for all your home wine making supplies. If you don’t see it, drop us a note or call us and we will find it for you. Check out our online store, we’ll ship same day if it’s in stock and your order is received before 3pm, or you can swing by and pick it up! Help us help you make the most of your wine making experience!
